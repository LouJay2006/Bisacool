About Cebu Waterfalls

Cebu’s waterfalls range from easy-access cascades to remote multi-tiered falls. This site collects practical info, photos, and travel tips for each location.

Why waterfalls matter

Waterfalls are not just scenic spots — they support local ecosystems, communities, and sustainable tourism. Respect the area: take trash, follow local guidelines, and consider hiring local guides.

Quick facts

  • Best months: November to May (less rain on trails)
  • Common access: day-hikes or short treks
  • Permit: sometimes required for protected areas

Our Mission

We provide accurate, up-to-date info to make waterfall trips safe and memorable while supporting local communities.
